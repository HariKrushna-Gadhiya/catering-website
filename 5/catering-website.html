FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order size? ▼
Our minimum order is for 50 guests for buffet service, and 30 guests for thali/plate service. For smaller gatherings, please call us to discuss options.
How far in advance should I book? ▼
For weddings, we recommend booking 3–6 months in advance. For other events, 2–4 weeks notice is ideal, though we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.
Do you offer tasting sessions? ▼
Yes! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for bookings of 200+ guests. For smaller events, a nominal tasting fee of ₹500 per person applies.
Is your food completely vegetarian? ▼
Yes, we serve 100% pure vegetarian food only. We also accommodate Jain menus (no root vegetables, no onion/garlic) and fasting-friendly menus upon request.
Do you provide crockery and serving staff? ▼
Yes, all our packages include cutlery, serving dishes, chafing dishes, and trained serving staff. Premium packages also include décor and event setup.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
Cancellations made 15+ days before the event receive a full refund. 7–14 days: 50% refund. Less than 7 days: no refund on the advance amount paid.
